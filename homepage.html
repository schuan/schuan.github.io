About

I am an Assistant Professor in Computer and Information Sciences Department at Fordham University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from The Ohio State University in August 2021. I was very fortunate to work with my advisors Professor Zhiqiang Lin and Professor Yinqian Zhang at Computer Security Laboratory (SecLab) . I received my M.E. degree from Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences and B.E.degree from University of Science and Technology of China.

Research Interests

  • Software Security
  • Programming Languages
  • Binary Analysis
  • Trusted Execution Environment
