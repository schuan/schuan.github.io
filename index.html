About

I am an Assitant Professor at Auburn University Computer Science Department. I am also a faculty member of Auburn Cyber Research Center (ACRC). I lead the Secure and Confidential Computing Lab at Auburn. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at The Ohio State University supervised by Professor Zhiqiang Lin and Professor Yinqian Zhang. I received my M.E. degree from Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences and B.E.degree from University of Science and Technology of China.

Research Interests

  • Software Security
  • Machine Learning Security
  • Hardware Security
